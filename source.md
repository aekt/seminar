## $\LaTeX$

Equations like this $\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx=\frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}$
should be _inline_.

They can also be _displayed_:
$$
 \frac{1}{\displaystyle 1+
   \frac{1}{\displaystyle 2+
   \frac{1}{\displaystyle 3+x}}} +
 \frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{2+\frac{1}{3+x}}}
$$
